<h1><strong>Parents Support Group (PSG)</strong></h1>
<p></p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The WRPS Parent Support Group (PSG) is a community of parent volunteers
who partner with the school to enrich the school experience and support
student development. PSG members work alongside teachers and school leaders
to contribute to a vibrant and caring school environment for all students.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>PSG members volunteer their time, talents, and ideas to support a range
of school events and activities, helping bring school initiatives to life.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Link to PSG website for sign up and more info: <a href="https://sites.google.com/view/psg-wrps/home" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://sites.google.com/view/psg-wrps/home</a>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
