<h1><strong>TIE (Trips for Internationalisation Experience)</strong></h1>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Develop Emerging 21st Century Competencies of global awareness and cross-cultural
skills and sensitivities in our students</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Expose students to the transformation and progress of Asia and around
the world, and sensitise them to the place of Singapore in the world; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide platforms and opportunities for students to work collaborative
and learn through real-life experiences and problem-solving, while communicating
their learning effectively and clearly to others.</p>
</li>
</ul>
