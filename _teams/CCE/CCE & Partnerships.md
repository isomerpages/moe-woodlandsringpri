<h4><strong>CCE → TIE &amp; Integration Programme</strong></h4>
<p><strong><u>TIE (Trips for Internationalisation Experience)</u></strong>
</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Develop Emerging 21st Century Competencies of global
awareness and cross-cultural skills and sensitivities in our students.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Expose students to the transformation and progress
of Asia and around the world, and sensitise them to the place of Singapore
in the world; and</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Provide platforms and opportunities for students to
work collaborative and learn through real-life experiences and problem-solving,
while communicating their learning effectively and clearly to Others.</p>
<p><strong><u>Integration Programme (SG Adventure: Exploring our Global Home)</u></strong>
</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To foster and deepen a sense of
identity and belonging to Singapore</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To foster better understanding and
appreciation of Singapore’s culture, history and heritage</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Forming friendships based on shared
interests</p>
