<p><strong>Good Character</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Have a sound moral compass and a strong sense of right and wrong, think
critically and ethically, be discerning in judgment, take responsibility
for choices and actions, be caring towards others and strive for excellence</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Resilience and Social-Emotional Well-Being</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Have a balanced sense of self, form healthy relationships, be resilient
when faced with challenges, find meaning in life, and have a sense of gratitude
and appreciation</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Future Readiness</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Have a sense of purpose in life, develop the dispositions of adaptability
and lifelong learning so as to be able to navigate education and career
pathways purposefully and take on the challenges of the future, including
the world of work and life</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Active Citizenship</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">

nation, a sense of hope in themselves and the future, an awareness of the
