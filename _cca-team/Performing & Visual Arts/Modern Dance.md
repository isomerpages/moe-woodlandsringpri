<h2>Overview:</h2>
<p>The Modern Dancers of Woodlands Ring Primary School&nbsp;are trained in
different genres of dances such as hip hop and contemporary. Our dancers
use their body movements to express their emotions, individuality and personal
style.</p>
<p>Our experienced and passionate instructors and teachers hope to inspire
the love of dance in our CCA members and help them develop confidence through
dance.</p>
<p>If you think you have good rhythm, timing, concentration and most importantly,
the determination to work hard, then you are definitely whom we are looking
for!</p>
