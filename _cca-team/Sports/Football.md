<h1>Football</h1>
<h2>Overview:</h2>
<p>The Football CCA is one of the popular CCAs in WRPS. Each year, we receive
many applications from students who are eager to join. The CCA aims to
develop our members not only physically but holistically as well. Our members
will get the opportunity to discover their own unique talents as well as
hone their personal set of skills. The Football CCA will help add value
to these members and ensure their development throughout their primary
school life.</p>
